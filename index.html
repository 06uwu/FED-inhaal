Falcon 9

Falcon 9 is a reusable, two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of satellites and the Dragon spacecraft into orbit and beyond.

Merlin Engines

Merlin is a family of rocket engines developed by SpaceX for use on its Falcon launch vehicles. Merlin engines use a rocket-grade kerosene (RP-1) and liquid oxygen (LOX) propellant mixture.
